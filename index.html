FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. Is it an online program?

A: No, it’s an offline program. You have to be available everyday at our office in Hubballi, Karnataka.

2. What type of Startups ideas will I be working on?

A: You will be working on a digital product in the field of Social Media, Entertainment, Advertisement & Education.

3. Will I be working on 1 idea or multiple ideas?

A: There will be an evaluation process, based on which you will be assigned a Startup idea to work on. For the next 6 months you along with other team members will be responsible to nurture and build the idea into a viable company.

4. What is the procedure of selection?

A: There will 2 rounds. The first round is a MCQ test focused on analysing you attitude. The first round is attached with the application. Shortlisted Candidates will be called to our office in Hubballi for the second round. In the second round, we will test your basic skills for entrepreneurship. After the second round, our team will shortlist the 9 best candidates will be shortlisted for the Program.

5. How will I get hands-on experience from this program?

A: Selected candidates will not just be given Basic knowledge and training but will also be assigned to internal Startup project, which has to nurtured and grown by the candidates in the period of next 6 months.

6. Will I receive any post program support from Small Town Startup team If I want to start my Startup?

A: There will be pitch day organised post the completion of the Program. You can pitch us your idea and if we love your idea, we will provide you early-stage investments plus access to our office and team in exchange of a share of your company.

7. Post the Program, I want to setup my Startup in a different city. Will the team at small town Startups provide me any assistance?

A: We may not able to provide all the services and support that we can give to the startups incubated with us in Hubli but we will provide you consultation & mentorship services during the early stages of your Startup journey.

8. Will I get any placement assistance from Small Town Startups?

A: If you’re skills match our expectations; we may hire you. If you are looking for a job elsewhere, the experience that you have gained will help you get job opportunity.

9. If I am relocating from a different city, will be provided assistance with accommodation?

A: We as a company don’t provide you any accommodation assistance, but our team members are always happy to help you to find a good place.

10. Do I need to have a pre-qualification to apply?

A: No, there is no pre-qualification is required. We are driven by skills and attitude, not by academical certifications.

11. How I need to have some certificates to match the eligibility criteria?

A: No! Your skills will be put to test during round 2 of the selection process.

12. Will I receive a certificate on completing this program?

A: Yes! You will receive a certificate plus a letter of appreciation highlighting your key achievements during the program.

13. Is there any Application fee?

A. There is no Application fee. Only the 9 selected candidates will have to pay the program fee.
